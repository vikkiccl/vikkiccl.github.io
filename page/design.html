CW1:

Index page: The now showing and up coming movies are ordered in an ordered list, as I think that it is clearer for users to distinguish one movie from another with numbers next to the movie.

Now showing page: A drop-down list is used to show the cinema and timeslot and hide other possible options in a clear design. The thumbnail, name of the movie and timeslots are wrapped in a form due to the information of these three elements will change at the same time if another movie is selected. The address and image of the map are located in the same paragraph with an id so both of them can be called with the id.

Ticket page: The seating plan of the cinema is made by a table, with a neat look and suitable for changing the elements to be clickable in later stage.

Print page: All information of a movie are contained in a form, with each item as a label.
